Duskmourn: House of Horror Card Search

Add Booster
Booster Strings must be in the following format: [weight]:[id 1],[id 2],... [id n] where [id n] is either c, u, r, w, f, l, or fl (indicating card type) followed by an integer (indicating number of that card in the booster).
c = random common card
u = random uncommon card
r = random rare card
w = random card
f = random foil card
l = random land card
fl = random foil land card
